What is happening to our planet?

Climate change is increasing the frequency and intensity of natural disasters. Rising temperatures, melting ice caps, and extreme weather events are affecting millions of lives worldwide.

Major Climate Disasters

Australia Wildfires (2019–2020)

Burned over 18 million hectares, killing billions of animals and destroying thousands of homes.

European Heatwave (2022)

Record-breaking temperatures caused thousands of heat-related deaths across Europe.

Pakistan Floods (2022)

One-third of the country was submerged, affecting over 30 million people.

Hurricane Katrina (2005)

One of the deadliest hurricanes in US history, causing massive destruction in New Orleans.

California Wildfires (2020)

Record-breaking fires due to extreme heat and drought conditions.

Amazon Rainforest Fires

Deforestation and fires threaten the world's largest carbon sink.

Rising Sea Levels

Coastal cities like Jakarta and Miami are at risk of being submerged.

Arctic Ice Melt

The Arctic is warming four times faster than the global average.
